Piano Speech Synthesis

11.12.2016

I spent a lot of my free time watching YouTube videos, and at some point I stumbled across this video of an art installation where they modified a piano to play sounds that resembled speech.

That is pretty frickin' cool (and somewhat creepy). After seeing this, I got to thinking, "I bet I could synthesize this same phenomenon on a computer." And thus, I had a final project for my Classical Mechanics class. After about 2 weeks worth of work in python, I had a product that I was (somewhat) happy with. Here's the video that explains the underlying physics and demonstrates the script.
